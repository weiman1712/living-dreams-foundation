About the Living Dreams Foundation

Living dreamsfoundation is a charity organisation that helps in empowering young out of school adoescent girls and women in acheiving their dreams and aspirations as they grow towards their full potential. It is a sole proprietorship organisation started by Wanirwoth Phionah in 2019 due to the high rate of teenage pregnancy during COVID-19 pandemic among the young girls in West Nile and picked interest in inspiring the young mothers and women through hand skill training, financial literacy and reproductive health. These activities help in strengthening access to labor markets, helps the young out of school adolescent girls and women to participate in in social economic activities, improves livelihood through creation of micro and small business enterprises and providing platforms for the young out of school adolescent girls and women to express their feelings.

  • Our Vision: A community with Empowered young adolescent girls sharing equal rights and opportunities.
  • Our Mission: To provide quality, sustainable, and flexible services that uphold human rights and create opportunities, empowering the young adolescent girls to live in harmony.
  • Our Aim: The goal of the organization is to promote trade and socio-economic empowerment of young adolescent girls.

Our Services

As above, we help the youth in Uganda to dream big and strive to leave their dreams and also help them to become the best they can be so that we they can be the next generation of ugandan leaders and become great men and women around Uganda.

Strengthening Access to the Labor Market:

We work tirelessly to enhance the employability of young adolescent girls within a few months or a short period of time.

Empowering Girls in Communities:

We empower young adolescent girls to participate in socio-economic activities within their communities.

Supporting Out-of-School Girls:

We improve the livelihoods of out-of-school adolescent girls by creating micro and small business enterprises.

Providing a Voice:

We provide a platform for young adolescent girls to express their feelings and aspirations.
